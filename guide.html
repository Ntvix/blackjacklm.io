Poker Rules

  • Two-player game: Player 1 vs Player 2.
  • Each player starts with 500 chips.
  • Click chips to place bets; each turn alternates between players.
  • Press Deal to draw 5 cards for each player.
  • Press Reveal to show all cards and determine the winner.
  • Hand rankings (best to worst): Straight Flush, Four of a Kind, Full House, Flush, Straight, Three of a Kind, Two Pair, Pair, High Card.
  • The winner takes the pot. In case of a tie, the pot is split evenly.

Blackjack Rules

  • One player vs dealer.
  • Goal: reach 21 or get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over.
  • Each player starts with 500 chips.
  • Place a bet by clicking chips.
  • Press Deal to get two cards for player and dealer (one dealer card face-down).
  • Use Hit to draw a card or Stand to keep your current hand.
  • If you go over 21, you bust and lose your bet.
  • Dealer reveals their hidden card and hits until reaching 17 or higher.
  • Winning hands pay 1:1, blackjack pays 3:2. Tie results in a push (bet returned).
